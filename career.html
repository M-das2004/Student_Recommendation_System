Technical & Vocational Courses

Technical and vocational education is an excellent path for gaining practical skills and securing stable employment. Here are some of the key government institutions and courses available.

Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs)

ITIs offer a wide range of short-term and long-term vocational courses. Some popular trades include:

  • Electrician
  • Fitter
  • Plumber
  • Welder
  • Computer Operator & Programming Assistant (COPA)
  • Stenography
  • Dress Making

Government Polytechnic Colleges

Polytechnics offer diploma courses for a strong foundation in technical fields. Common diploma courses include:

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Automobile Engineering

Government Job & Civil Service Exams

A career in government service offers stability and opportunities to serve the community. The main recruitment bodies for the Union Territory of J&K are JKSSB and JKPSC.

JK Services Selection Board (JKSSB)

JKSSB conducts exams for various non-gazetted posts across departments. Common job profiles include:

  • Junior Assistant
  • Patwari
  • Accounts Assistant
  • Junior Engineer (JE)
  • Stenographer
  • Forester

JK Public Service Commission (JKPSC)

JKPSC is responsible for recruiting candidates for gazetted posts and civil services through its competitive examinations. Key exams and posts include:

  • J&K Combined Competitive Examination (CCE) for KAS
  • Assistant Conservator of Forests (ACF)
  • Judicial Service Exams
  • Assistant Engineer
  • Lecturer
